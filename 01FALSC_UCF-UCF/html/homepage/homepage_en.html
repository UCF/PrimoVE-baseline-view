Welcome to UCF's new Discovery tool.

What's in Primo? Primo provides one-stop searching for the catalog, e-books, videos, articles, and more.

Primo Searching Basics. Start typing a search and Primo will show options for searching Everything, the Library Collection, and more. Use the guides beneath the search box to pick specific fields or item types. Further refine your search by selecting options on the left side of the results screen. Finally, click on titles in the results list to see items availability and options for getting the full text.

See the Primo Guides for more instruction and help.

Primo does more!

  • View and renew checked-out items
  • Save, email, and export citations
  • Request books from UCF's ARC, and borrow books and articles from other libraries.
